Shared Goals. Shared Success.

At VSHFREIGHT, we’ve worked hard to earn a reputation for advocating equally for our customers and carriers. We pride ourselves on building lasting relationships that match the capacity needs and service expectations of our shippers with the capability and productivity goals of our carrier network.

We’ll take the time to understand your needs and work collaboratively to reduce empty miles while finding consistent, quality loads that will increase productivity and profitability for your business. As a VSHFREIGHT carrier, you join one of the leading and fastest-growing transportation and logistics organizations in the industry.
